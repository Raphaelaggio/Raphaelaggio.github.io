Welcome to my website

Hi! My name is Raphael Aggio and I would like to welcome you to my website. I am a Brazilian biologist/electronic technitian working on bioinformatics applied to metabolomics and systems biology. In the last couple of years I have developed few computer tools to analyse and interpret metabolomics data. Therefore, I have developed this simple website in order to store these computer tools and share my scientific achievements. Feel free to brownse around and please contact me if you have any type of feedback.

During these years in science I realised that collaboration is the key word to increase both quality and productivity. Thus, I am opened for collaborations on new computer tools and/or on improving the tools I have developed.

Cheers.

Raphael Aggio
